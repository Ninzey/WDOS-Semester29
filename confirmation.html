About us

  • Our main responsibility is to monitor and try to conserve the endangered turtles. We are using advanced remote technologies such as satallite waves, radio waves to detect them.

    Therefore, by learning more about sea turtles through this website, we hope to strengthen the world fund to protect the global turtle population.

Our Goals

  • As the Department of Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management, protecting sea turtles that come to our beaches is one of our main priorities.

    Treating injured turtles and breeding them to maintain the turtle population are also some of our goals.
